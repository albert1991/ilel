Research

Our research focuses on optimization and AI for logistics systems operating under energy, sustainability, and infrastructure constraints.

Energy-Responsive Logistics Systems

Routing, planning, and control of logistics operations under grid congestion, charging constraints, and coupled logistics–energy decisions.

Sustainable Transport Management

Electric and hydrogen vehicle routing, robust transport planning, asset utilization, and real-time operational control.

Stochastic Optimization & AI

Markov decision processes, multi-stage optimization, reinforcement learning, and predict-then-optimize approaches with recourse.
