Current projects

EnerLogiX — Energy-aware electric trucking

Methods and decision support for battery-electric trucking under real-world infrastructure constraints, linking routing and charging decisions with local energy production, storage management, and grid congestion.

Themes: BEV routing & charging · grid constraints · integrated logistics–energy planning

AI4ROAD — AI-driven planning & control for transport operations

Data-driven and optimization-based tooling to support planning, dispatching, and operational control in logistics practice, combining fast heuristics with learning-based components for robust performance.

Themes: control tower tooling · OR–ML heuristics · real-time decision-making

Scenario Predict-then-Optimize / Recourse MPC tooling

A methodology for decision-making under uncertainty that couples prediction and optimization with explicit recourse: scenario generation, recourse approximation with ML, and scalable optimization/heuristic layers.

Themes: stochastic optimization · scenario methods · recourse learning

VENI — Dynamic decision-making in logistics systems

Research on sequential and adaptive logistics decisions under uncertainty, including revision policies, multi-stage planning, and Markov decision process formulations with scalable solution approaches.

Themes: MDP/RL · adaptive planning · multi-stage models

Hydrogen transport & distribution networks

Optimization and planning models for hydrogen logistics, including infrastructure design, network operations, and the coupling between production locations, transport modes, and end-use demand.

Themes: network design · sustainable fuels · infrastructure planning

FTL-PDPC — Pickup-and-delivery with configurations

Algorithmic research on a full-truckload pickup-and-delivery problem with configuration decisions, focusing on scalable heuristics and computational benchmarking for real planning settings.

Themes: rich routing · large neighborhood search · benchmarking
