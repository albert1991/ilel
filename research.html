Research lines

Energy-responsive logistics systems

  • Battery-electric truck routing under grid congestion
  • Charging infrastructure planning and control
  • Integrated logistics–energy planning
  • Hydrogen transport and distribution networks

Sustainable transport management

  • Electric and hydrogen vehicle routing
  • Reducing empty mileage & improving utilization
  • Robust and stochastic transport planning
  • Real-time transport control

Stochastic optimization & AI

  • Markov decision processes & reinforcement learning
  • Stochastic and multi-stage optimization
  • Predict-then-optimize and recourse models
  • Hybrid OR–ML heuristics

Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews (2022)

A Green Hydrogen Energy System — Optimal control strategies for integrated hydrogen storage and power generation with wind energy

MDP Hydrogen Wind

Optimal operating policies for an integrated wind-to-hydrogen system, using Markov decision process modeling to manage uncertainty in renewable production and storage decisions.

Transportation Research Part E (2023)

Reliable Reserve-Crew Scheduling for Airlines

Robustness Scheduling Disruptions

A disruption-aware framework for reserve-crew planning that preserves operational flexibility and reduces downstream disruption costs through robust repair and scheduling strategies.

Journal of Supply Chain Management Science (2023)

A share-first-plan-second policy for efficient cooperation in a multi-modal transportation corridor

Collaboration Multimodal Policy

A practical collaboration protocol that achieves most of the benefits of joint planning while remaining implementable when full information sharing or centralized planning is infeasible.

Working paper / portal listing

Optimal Fulfillment and Replenishment for Omnichannel Retailers with Standard Shipping Contracts

MDP Inventory Omnichannel

Joint fulfillment and replenishment decision-making under realistic shipping contracts, modeled as a sequential decision problem to control cost and service trade-offs.
